Core Capabilities

Industrial Lining Services

Acid Tank Rubber Lining

Specialized protective lining for chemical storage tanks. Designed to withstand highly corrosive environments, ensuring zero chemical degradation and extending the lifespan of your critical storage infrastructure.

MS Water Pipe Lining

Premium internal and external rubber lining for Mild Steel (MS) pipes. Prevents rust and mineral build-up in water treatment and transport systems, guaranteeing long-term flow efficiency.

Rubber & Ceramic Lagging

Advanced lagging solutions including standard diamond rubber and high-performance ceramic tiles. Engineered to eliminate belt slippage, maximize grip in wet conditions, and provide extreme wear resistance for heavy-duty conveyor systems.
